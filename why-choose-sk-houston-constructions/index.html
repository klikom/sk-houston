Your Remodeling Questions Answered

Common questions our Houston clients ask about their renovation projects

How long does a typical remodel take?

Timelines vary by project scope. A bathroom might take 3-6 weeks, while a kitchen or addition can take 6-12 weeks. We provide a detailed project schedule after the planning phase.

How much will my remodel cost?

Costs depend entirely on the size, materials, and complexity. We provide a detailed, itemized estimate after our free initial consultation so you know exactly what to expect.

Do I need to move out during the renovation?

Often, you can remain in your home, especially for kitchen or bath remodels. For large-scale renovations or additions, we'll discuss the best approach for your comfort and safety during the planning stage.

How do you handle unexpected issues?

We build in contingency planning. If unforeseen issues arise (like hidden plumbing problems), we communicate immediately, explain the options, and get your approval before proceeding with any changes to scope or cost.
